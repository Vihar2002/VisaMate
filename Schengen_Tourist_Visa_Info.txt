SECTION 1 · PURPOSE & VALIDITY
A Schengen **Uniform Short-Stay Visa (Type C)** allows travel for tourism, family visits, or short business within the 27 Schengen States **up to 90 days in any rolling 180-day period**.  
Multiple entry is possible if “need to travel frequently” is justified (e.g., property ownership, family ties, business).

SECTION 2 · DOCUMENT CHECKLIST (CORE)
1. **Passport** valid for at least three months after intended departure, issued within the past 10 years, with ≥ 2 blank pages.  
2. **Completed application form** (signed).  
3. **One recent photograph** (35 mm × 45 mm) on white background.  
4. **Travel itinerary**: confirmed round-trip flight reservation or proof of onward travel.  
5. **Proof of accommodation**: hotel bookings, rental agreement, or invitation letter + host ID.  
6. **Proof of financial means**:  
   • Minimum €50–70 per travel day (exact threshold differs by member state).  
   • Acceptable evidence: 3-month bank statements, payslips, traveller’s cheques, or sponsor letter.  
7. **Travel medical insurance** with minimum coverage of **€30 000**, valid for the entire Schengen area and full duration of stay.  
8. **Visa fee payment receipt** (see Section 4).

SECTION 3 · OPTIONAL / SUPPORTING DOCUMENTS
• Employment certificate or study enrolment letter to demonstrate ties to home country.  
• Property deeds, marriage certificate, or family register if relevant.  
• Previous Schengen visas or entry stamps to prove compliance history.

SECTION 4 · FEES & DISCOUNTS  (effective Jan 2024)
• **Adults (12 +)** — €80  
• **Children aged 6–11** — €40  
• **Children < 6 years** — free  
• Nationals of Armenia, Azerbaijan, and Russia: €35 under visa-facilitation agreements.  
Fees are non-refundable once the application is lodged.

SECTION 5 · APPLICATION TIMELINE
1. Earliest application: **6 months** before travel (9 months for seafarers).  
2. Latest recommended: **at least 15 calendar days** before departure.  
3. Submit online or paper application + book **biometric appointment** (fingerprints + photo).  
4. Standard decision time: **15 calendar days**, extendable to 45 days if further scrutiny is needed.

SECTION 6 · BIOMETRICS & VIS REGISTER
Fingerprints taken once every 59 months and stored in the Visa Information System (VIS).  
Applicants who provided biometrics within the last 5 years may be exempt unless prints are of poor quality.

SECTION 7 · CONDITIONS & LIMITATIONS
• **No work** (paid or unpaid) allowed.  
• Visa does **not** guarantee entry; final decision lies with the border officer.  
• Over-stays trigger fines, entry bans, and visa refusals; ensure compliance with **90/180 rule**.  
• Travel insurance must be valid for the **entire Schengen zone** and every intended entry.

SECTION 8 · MULTIPLE ENTRY CRITERIA
A 1-, 3-, or 5-year multiple-entry visa may be granted if:  
• The applicant held two visas in the preceding two years and used them lawfully **or**  
• Has demonstrable need for frequent travel (business, family).  
Each stay is still capped at 90/180 days.

SECTION 9 · COMMON GROUNDS FOR REFUSAL
• Insufficient financial means or unexplained large deposits.  
• Ambiguous itinerary or lack of accommodation proof.  
• Missing or invalid travel insurance.  
• Doubt over intention to return (weak ties, prior overstays).  
• Presentation of forged or incomplete documents.
