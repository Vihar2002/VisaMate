# UNITED KINGDOM  —  STUDENT VISA  (Student Route, Tier 4)
# Source Consolidation Date: 2025-04-10

#   • UKVI Caseworker Guidance – Student Route (version 16.0)
#   • Immigration Rules Appendix Student  (Feb 2025)

SECTION 1 · PURPOSE & VALIDITY
A UK Student Visa allows full-time study in the UK for courses longer than 6 months.
Visa length = course length + 4 additional months (≤ PhD) or +2 months (sub-degree).
Holders may work **up to 20 hours/week** in term time and full-time in vacation.

SECTION 2 · DOCUMENT CHECKLIST
1. **CAS (Confirmation of Acceptance for Studies)** reference number issued by the sponsoring institution.  
2. **Passport** valid for the duration of stay + one blank page.  
3. **Financial proof**:  
   • Tuition fees for first year (minus any paid deposit).  
   • **Living funds** held for ≥ 28 days:  
     – **£1 334 per month** (up to 9 months) if studying in Greater London.  
     – **£1 023 per month** if outside London.  
   • Allowed formats: bank statements, loan letter, or official sponsorship letter.  
4. **English language evidence**: IELTS, Pearson PTE, or UKVI-approved SELT meeting CEFR B2 (degree level) or B1 (below degree).  
5. **Tuberculosis (TB) test certificate** if resident of a listed country.  
6. **ATAS certificate** (where required for sensitive courses).  
7. **Parental consent** (if applicant is under 18).

SECTION 3 · FEES & SURCHARGES
• **Visa application fee**: **£490** (outside UK) or £490 (in-country switch/extend).  
• **Immigration Health Surcharge (IHS)**: **£776 per year** of visa validity, paid up-front.  
• Priority visa upgrade (optional): £500 (5-day), £1 000 (next-day) — availability varies.

SECTION 4 · APPLICATION TIMELINE
1. **Earliest application**: 6 months before course start (out-of-country) or 3 months (in-country).  
2. Complete online application and pay fees.  
3. Upload supporting documents or attend **UKVCAS / VAC** for scanning.  
4. Attend biometric appointment (fingerprints + photo).  
5. Average decision time: **3 weeks** (standard service), excluding shipping of BRP.  
6. Collect Biometric Residence Permit (BRP) within 10 days of arrival.

SECTION 5 · WORK CONDITIONS
• **Term-time limit**: 20 hours/week for degree level and above, 10 hours/week for below degree.  
• **Vacation**: full-time work permitted.  
• No self-employment, no professional sport, no permanent positions.  
• Sandwich-placement courses: up to 50% of course duration may be work placement if integral.

SECTION 6 · DEPENDANTS
• Allowed for postgraduate courses ≥ 9 months at HESA-registered providers or government-sponsored students.  
• Dependants must show £845/month (London) or £680/month (outside) for living costs.

SECTION 7 · EXTENSIONS & POST-STUDY ROUTES
• Switch to Graduate Visa for 2 years (3 years for PhD) within UK before student visa expires.  
• CAS required for extensions of continued study.

SECTION 8 · COMMON REFUSAL REASONS
• Insufficient maintenance funds or funds not held for full 28-day period.  
• CAS mismatch (course dates, fees, or sponsorship status).  
• Invalid or expired TB certificate.  
• Incomplete documentary evidence in formats specified by UKVI.
